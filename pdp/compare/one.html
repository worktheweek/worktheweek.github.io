DESCRIPTION

Indulge in the classic warmth and beauty of this simple, yet elegant solitaire 2mm wide 14 karat yellow gold engagement ring. The yellow tone of these ring makes it a luscious backdrop for a center diamond. This timeless metal is more affordable than platinum, and it’s one of the most popular selections at Shane Co.

A ring that evokes the happiness of love and partnership is even more joyful when you know that it was sustainably sourced and mindfully produced. From our mission to our unique jewelry designs, everything at Shane Co is held to exceptional standards.
